Body Pump

Body Pump is the original weights class that builds strength, tones your body, and pushes you to the limit every time.

Yoga

Yoga is a spiritual discipline which includes breath control, meditation, and the adoption of specific postures to aid health and relaxation.

Hitt

Hitt is a fitness class that incorporates cardio, resistance training and body conditioning.

Meditation

Meditation classes will allow you to unwind your mind and return home relaxed and refreshed. They will provide you with new tools for dealing with stress, anxiety, and problems.

Boddy Attack

Boddy Attack is an intense 45 minute, sports inspired cardio workout aimed at building both your strength and stamina.

Plates

Spin

Spin is a a structured group cycle workout that combines hills, flats, mountain peaks, and time trials. The intervals and peaks of the rides to the rhythm of powerful music and increases cardiovascular fitness, leg strength and muscular endurance.
